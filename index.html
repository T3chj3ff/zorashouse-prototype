Our Three Core Pillars

Everything we do is designed to provide you with what you need to thrive personally and professionally.

Space

Our beautiful Columbus Commons sanctuary provides coworking, shared offices, and private Z-Suites for connection and deep work.

Skills

We build leaders. Through our Fellowship program and Wealth Builders initiative, we offer transformative skill-building cohorts.

Strategic Connections

From Sunday Dinners to our WoCO Market, we facilitate lifelong mentorships, collaborations, and sisterhood.
